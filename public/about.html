About MediBook

MediBook is a modern healthcare appointment scheduling platform designed to streamline the process of booking medical consultations.

Easy Scheduling

Book appointments with just a few clicks, choose your preferred time slots, and manage your bookings effortlessly.

Appointment History

Keep track of all your past appointments and maintain a comprehensive medical visit history.

Smart Notifications

Receive timely reminders and updates about your upcoming appointments.
